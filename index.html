About Me

Hey! I'm an undergraduate Physics student at IIT Kanpur (Class of 27'), with a focus on CFD & FEA, Computational/Numerical Methods and Aerodynamics

(14th) Inter-IIT Tech Meet, 2025 -
Wind-tunnel testing

from L to R: Saket, Abhivardhan, Dhruv, Mohit(Me), Junaid
Absentees: Keerthana, Vivek, Aniket

I'm the Aerodynamics-head for the Institute Formula SAE team. Over the past year, I designed & simulated the team's first aerodynamics package in over 5 years: handling the concept, development, geometry and CFD analysis end-to-end.

As part of IIT Kanpur's high-prep team for LAT Aerospace PS at the 14th InterIIT Tech Meet, I worked on wing design as well as CFD.
We placed 3rd, contributing to IIT Kanpur's overall 2nd place finish.

My related coursework includes: Fluid Mechanics & rate processes, Incompressible Aerodynamics, Finite Element Methods for Fluid Dynamics, High-Performance Computing, Computational Physics and more.

Outside academics, I like sketching & (sometimes)painting, exploring music across genres and watching my favorite films & series.
